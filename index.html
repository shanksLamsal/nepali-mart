Our Story

Nepali-Mart traces its roots back to 1978, when visionary entrepreneur Ram Prasad Nepal opened a small general store in the bustling alleys of Ason, Kathmandu. What began as a tiny shop selling rice, salt, and lentils soon became a cornerstone for local families, thanks to Ram Prasad's relentless focus on fair trade practices, supporting local farmers, and ensuring product authenticity.

Throughout the 1980s and 1990s, Nepali-Mart earned recognition for being among the first in Nepal to standardize pricing on essential goods — an initiative applauded by local consumer rights groups at the time. Critics and analysts often noted how Nepali-Mart helped reduce reliance on imported packaged goods, instead prioritizing partnerships with homegrown producers from places like Jhapa, Ilam, and Mustang.

By 2000, Nepali-Mart had evolved into a chain of modern retail stores, known for its clean shopping environments, transparent pricing, and strict quality control standards. In 2008, we were featured in Nepal Business Review for introducing eco-friendly packaging and supporting rural women's cooperatives.

In 2014, we launched our online shopping platform, becoming one of the earliest adopters of e-commerce for everyday goods in Nepal. Today, Nepali-Mart is celebrated for:

Championing Nepali-made products

Over 60% of our inventory is sourced from Nepali farmers and manufacturers.

Transparent pricing and ethical sourcing

Key principles that have defined our business for over 45 years.

Social impact

From fair wages to disaster relief initiatives, we are deeply woven into the fabric of our community.

At Nepali-Mart, we don't just sell goods — we continue a legacy of trust, tradition, and transformation.

Our Journey

1978

Nepali-Mart is founded in Ason, Kathmandu by Ram Prasad Nepal as a family-run shop for daily essentials.

1985

Expanded to 3 outlets across the Kathmandu Valley, introducing transparent pricing and local sourcing agreements.

1992

Recognized by Consumer Rights Group Nepal for fair trade practices.

2000

Opened our 10th location and launched in-store quality control labs.

2008

Switched to eco-friendly packaging and began partnerships with women's cooperatives for handmade products.

2014

Nepali-Mart Online is launched — one of Nepal's first mart-to-door e-commerce platforms.

2016

Won National E-Commerce Innovation Award.

2021

Crossed 50,000 online customers and expanded delivery coverage nationwide.

Today

Nepali-Mart continues to lead as a trusted retailer blending tradition with modern convenience.
