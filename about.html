About Nepali Mart

Nepali Mart is your trusted neighborhood store, inspired by the spirit of Bhatbhateni Supermarket. We are dedicated to bringing you the best quality products at the most affordable prices. Our store is a one-stop-shop for all your daily needs, from fresh groceries to household essentials.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with a convenient and enjoyable shopping experience, offering a wide range of products while maintaining the highest standards of quality and customer service. We aim to be a cornerstone of the community, fostering a sense of trust and reliability.

Our Location

You can find us in the heart of Kathmandu, Nepal. We are open every day from 8 AM to 9 PM.
